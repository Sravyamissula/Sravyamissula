About Mexico

Mexico, officially the United Mexican States, is a vibrant and culturally rich country located in North America, bordered by the United States to the north, and Belize and Guatemala to the south. Known for its ancient civilizations, stunning landscapes, and diverse traditions, Mexico offers a blend of history, modernity, and natural beauty.

Famous Places In Mexico

Historical Places in Mexico

Chichén Itzá (Yucatán)

A major Mayan city that was once one of the largest and most influential in Mesoamerica.

Teotihuacán (State of Mexico)

An ancient city built by an unknown civilization around 200 BCE.The Pyramid of the Sun and the Pyramid of the Moon are the most famous structures, demonstrating advanced engineering and urban planning.

Palenque (Chiapas)

An ancient Mayan city nestled in the jungles of southern Mexico.Famous for the Temple of Inscriptions, which houses the tomb of the great ruler Pakal.

Monte Albán (Oaxaca)

An ancient Zapotec city located atop a mountain, overlooking the valley of Oaxaca.

Tulum (Quintana Roo)

A walled Mayan city located on the Caribbean coast, offering stunning views of the sea.

Customs and Traditions of Mexico

Día de los Muertos (Day of the Dead)

Creating altars (ofrendas) decorated with photos, marigolds, candles, and the favorite items of the deceased.

Las Posadas

Processions with candles and songs, going from house to house.

Quinceañera

The girl wears a lavish gown and receives symbolic gifts, such as a tiara or cross.
